The MAF is a pioneering organization dedicated to revolutionizing the educational and medical landscapes. Guided by the principles of selflessness and inclusivity, MAF envisions a transformative impact on society through innovative methods and collaborative efforts.

Contact Us

In addition to its vital work in combating school dropouts, MAF is actively involved in a wide array of activities aimed at fostering holistic community development. These encompass employment programs for youth and women, the protection and advocacy of citizens' rights, healthcare initiatives MAF firmly believes in the power of education as a catalyst for change, and as such, they work diligently to ensure that education reaches the most underserved segments of society.

The Mission Awareness Foundation (MAF) is a non-profit societal organization with a noble mission at its core. MAF is a Secular Trust registered in 2020 with the Charity Commissioner of Mumbai. Founded in the year 2020 by a dedicated group of well-educated and socially responsible individuals, MAF is committed to making a positive impact on society. The organization passionately pursues a range of development programs aimed at uplifting communities that have been socially, economically, and educationally marginalized.

MAF's tireless efforts extend to various areas of social concern, with a primary focus on eradicating school dropouts, through a combination of awareness campaigns, Scholarships and practical initiatives, MAF strives to create an environment where every child has the opportunity to pursue their dreams and reach their full potential.

Vision:

  • The vision of MAF is to become a catalyst for the progression, enhancement.

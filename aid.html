Aid for needy and maritorious students

The National Eligibility cum Entrance Test (NEET) is India’s main exam for admissions into medical and dental programs, with around 1.5 million test-takers annually. Due to its competitive nature, students often retake NEET to improve scores and secure spots in reputed colleges, provided they meet the age limit of 25 years.

Reason for not scoring well in the first attempt:

  • State board and NCERT syllabus.
  • No effective study plan and timetable.
  • Confusion about the right study material.
  • Lack of practice and regular problem-solving.
  • Not enough time devoted per day to prepare for physics, chemistry and Biology.
  • Distraction due to some personal, family or health reason.
  • Felt low many times; hence gave up too easily
